<p><p>Centrifugal draught fans are crucial for various industrial applications, categorized into three main types: radial, backward, and forward fans. Radial fans provide high-pressure airflow suitable for ventilation systems. Backward fans are designed for high efficiency and lower energy consumption, ideal for handling dust-laden air. Forward fans offer higher airflow rates at lower pressures, commonly used in HVAC systems. Each type serves distinct operational needs, impacting their efficiency, performance, and suitability for different environments and applications.</p></p>
