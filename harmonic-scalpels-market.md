<p><p>The Harmonic Scalpels market is primarily divided into two types: Attracting Harmonic Scalpels and Cutting Harmonic Scalpels. Attracting Harmonic Scalpels utilize ultrasonic energy to create a gentle grasping force, facilitating tissue handling and enhancing precision during procedures. In contrast, Cutting Harmonic Scalpels focus on tissue cutting through high-frequency vibrations, allowing for effective dissection while minimizing thermal damage. Both types are crucial in surgical settings, promoting efficient operations and improved patient outcomes by providing enhanced control and precision for surgeons.</p></p>
