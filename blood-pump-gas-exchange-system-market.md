<p><p>The Blood Pump Gas Exchange System market comprises two main types: Single Roller Pump Systems and Double Roller Pump Systems. Single Roller Pumps utilize a single set of rollers to propel blood through tubing, offering simplicity and reliability, suitable for various medical applications. In contrast, Double Roller Pumps feature two sets of rollers, providing enhanced precision and flow control, which is crucial for complex procedures. Both systems are essential in maintaining efficient gas exchange during blood treatment and transfusion processes.</p></p>
