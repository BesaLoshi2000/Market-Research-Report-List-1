<p><p>The fluoro polymer film market comprises several key types, each with distinct properties and applications. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is renowned for its excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability. PVF (Polyvinyl Fluoride) offers good mechanical properties and is often used in protective coatings. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) is valued for its durability and resistance to UV radiation. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) combines chemical resistance with flexibility. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) provides high thermal stability, while ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) is known for its strength and transparency, catering to various industrial needs. Other types include specialized formulations for niche applications.</p></p>
