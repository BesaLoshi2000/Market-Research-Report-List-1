<p><p>The water knife cutting machine market features primarily two types: sand water knives and pure water knives. Sand water knives use a mixture of water and abrasive materials, ideal for cutting through hard materials like metal and stone, delivering precision and efficiency. On the other hand, pure water knives utilize only high-pressure water, suitable for softer materials such as rubber and food products, ensuring clean cuts without contaminants. Both types cater to specific industrial applications, enhancing operational versatility.</p></p>
