NaK filled melt pressure transducers utilize a sodium-potassium alloy, offering high thermal conductivity and responsiveness, ideal for precise measurements in extreme temperatures. In contrast, oil-filled melt pressure transducers use hydraulic oil for better sensitivity and stability in pressure readings. Both types are crucial for optimizing processing conditions and ensuring product quality in applications such as plastics and polymer manufacturing.</p></p>
