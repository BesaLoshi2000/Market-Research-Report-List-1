<p><strong>The Axial Bone Densitometry Measurement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA)</li><li>Quantitative Computed Tomography (QCT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Axial bone densitometry measurement includes two primary market types: Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) and Quantitative Computed Tomography (QCT). DEXA is the most widely used technique, providing precise measurements of bone mineral density through low-dose X-rays, primarily for diagnosing osteoporosis. QCT, on the other hand, utilizes computed tomography to assess bone density in three dimensions, offering detailed insights into bone quality and density variations. Both techniques play crucial roles in evaluating bone health and fracture risk.</p></p>
