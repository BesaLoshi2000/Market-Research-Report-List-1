<p><p>The Porcelain Fused to Metal (PFM) dental bridge market consists of several types tailored for specific dental needs. Traditional bridges involve two crowns on adjacent teeth supporting a middle pontic. Cantilever bridges are anchored to just one side, making them suitable for limited space. Maryland bridges use a framework with metal or porcelain wings that bond to back teeth, providing a minimally invasive option. Implant-supported bridges rely on dental implants for stability, offering a robust solution for extensive tooth loss.</p></p>
