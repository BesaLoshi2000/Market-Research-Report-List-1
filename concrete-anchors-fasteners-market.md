<p><strong>The Concrete Anchors Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Anchors or Fasteners</li><li>Adhesive Anchors or Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete anchors fasteners market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical anchors and adhesive anchors. Mechanical anchors use physical interlocking mechanisms, such as expansion or abrasion, to create a secure hold in concrete. These are suitable for heavy loads and immediate loading conditions. In contrast, adhesive anchors utilize epoxy or resin to bond with the concrete, providing strong support over time, especially in non-cracked conditions. Each type serves specific applications based on load requirements and environmental factors.</p></p>
