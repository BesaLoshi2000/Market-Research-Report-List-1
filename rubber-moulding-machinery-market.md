<p><p>The rubber moulding machinery market is primarily divided into two types: semi-automatic and automatic rubber moulding machinery. Semi-automatic rubber moulding machinery requires some manual intervention during the moulding process, allowing operators to control specific functions while maintaining production efficiency. In contrast, automatic rubber moulding machinery operates independently with minimal human involvement, utilizing advanced technology for precise control and high output. Both types cater to varying production needs, with automatic machinery typically preferred for large-scale and consistent manufacturing operations.</p></p>
