<p><p>The Percutaneous Coronary Intervention (PCI) market encompasses various key products aimed at treating coronary artery diseases. Coronary stents are used to keep arteries open post-angioplasty. PTCA catheters facilitate the inflation of balloons to widen narrowed arteries. Coronary guidewires assist in navigating the coronary vasculature during procedures. Embolic protection devices are employed to capture debris during interventions, minimizing complications. Together, these products enhance the safety and efficacy of PCI, addressing the growing demand for minimally invasive cardiac treatments.</p></p>
