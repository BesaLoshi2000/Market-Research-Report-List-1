<p><p>The emergency shut-off valve market features primarily two types: motorised valves and solenoid valves. Motorised valves utilize electric motors to operate and provide precise control over fluid flow, making them suitable for large-scale industrial applications. Solenoid valves, on the other hand, use electromagnetic coils to actuate the valve, offering rapid response times and compact designs, ideal for automated systems. Both types are critical for ensuring safety and quick response in emergencies across various industries, including oil and gas, chemicals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
