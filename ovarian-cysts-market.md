<p><p>The ovarian cysts market is divided into two main types: functional and non-functional cysts. Functional cysts are the most common and develop during the menstrual cycle, including follicular and corpus luteum cysts; these typically resolve on their own. Non-functional cysts, on the other hand, arise from other underlying issues and include dermoid, endometriomas, and cystadenomas. The differentiation between these types is crucial for diagnosis and treatment, influencing both patient management and market demand for diagnostic and therapeutic solutions.</p></p>
