<p><p>Mobile app analytics software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on the vendor’s servers, allowing users to access data from anywhere with internet connectivity. This model typically offers scalability and automatic updates. Conversely, on-premise solutions are installed locally on a company’s servers, providing enhanced security and control over data. While on-premise may require more maintenance and upfront investment, it is preferred by organizations with strict compliance requirements.</p></p>
