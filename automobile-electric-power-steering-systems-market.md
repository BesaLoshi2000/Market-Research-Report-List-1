<p><strong>The Automobile Electric Power Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li><li>REPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems encompass three main types: Column Electric Power Steering (CEPS), Pinion Electric Power Steering (PEPS), and Rack Electric Power Steering (REPS). CEPS integrates the electric motor into the steering column, enhancing space efficiency, while PEPS places the motor at the pinion gear for improved responsiveness. REPS features the motor directly on the steering rack, offering faster and more precise steering feedback. These systems enhance vehicle handling, fuel efficiency, and overall driving comfort.</p></p>
