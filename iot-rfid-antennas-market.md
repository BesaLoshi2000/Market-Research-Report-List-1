<p><strong>The IoT RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li><li>Semi-Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID antennas market encompasses three primary types: passive, active, and semi-active RFID. Passive RFID relies on external readers for power, making it cost-effective and ideal for tracking items. Active RFID features its own power source, enabling longer ranges and real-time tracking, suitable for higher-value items. Semi-active RFID combines aspects of both, utilizing a battery to power sensors while still relying on readers for communication, offering a balance of range and affordability for various applications in industries like logistics and retail.</p></p>
