<p><p>The all-electric propulsion satellites market is categorized into three primary types based on orbit: Low Earth Orbit (LEO), Medium Earth Orbit (MEO), and Geosynchronous Earth Orbit (GEO). LEO satellites are typically used for communication and Earth observation, benefiting from reduced launch costs and shorter latency. MEO satellites are ideal for navigation and broadband services, balancing coverage and latency. GEO satellites, positioned significantly higher, provide stable communication and broadcasting services, making them essential for global connectivity and data transfer.</p></p>
