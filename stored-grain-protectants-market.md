<p><ul><li>Chemical Control</li><li>Physical Control</li><li>Biological Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stored grain protectants market is categorized into three main types: chemical, physical, and biological control. Chemical control involves the use of synthetic pesticides to eliminate pests and diseases affecting stored grains. Physical control focuses on non-chemical methods, such as temperature regulation, moisture management, and physical barriers to deter infestations. Biological control utilizes natural predators or microbial agents to manage pest populations, promoting eco-friendly practices. Together, these approaches enhance grain preservation and ensure food safety in storage facilities.</p></p>
