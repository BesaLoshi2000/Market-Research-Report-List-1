<p><p>The capacitance meter market is divided into two main types: non-bridge meters and bridge meters. In contrast, bridge meters are more sophisticated instruments that employ a balancing technique to achieve accurate capacitance measurements, suitable for precision applications and laboratory settings. Both types cater to various industries, including electronics, manufacturing, and research, reflecting the diverse needs for capacitance testing and measurement solutions.</p></p>
