<p><p>Hotel Revenue Management Software is categorized into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and lower upfront costs. They facilitate real-time data access and updates, making them ideal for properties with multiple locations. On-Premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on a hotel's servers, providing greater control over data security and customizable features but often involves higher initial investment and maintenance costs.</p></p>
