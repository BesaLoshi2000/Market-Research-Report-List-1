<p><p>Bellows seal control valves are essential for precision flow control in various industries, and they come in three primary types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual valves require operator intervention, providing simplicity and reliability for low-frequency adjustments. Pneumatic valves use compressed air for actuation, enabling rapid response and automation in dynamic processes. Electric valves employ electric actuators for precise control, ideal for applications needing advanced automation and integration with control systems. Each type caters to specific operational needs and preferences.</p></p>
