<p><ul><li>Bag and Cartridge Filter</li><li>Activated Carbon Filter</li><li>Media Filter</li><li>Sediment Filter</li><li>Reverse-Osmosis Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water-filtration unit market consists of various types, each serving distinct purposes. Bag and cartridge filters capture contaminants through physical barriers. Activated carbon filters absorb impurities and odors, enhancing taste. Media filters use layers of filtration media to remove particles and sediments effectively. Sediment filters target larger particles to protect downstream equipment. Reverse-osmosis filters utilize a semi-permeable membrane to eliminate dissolved solids and contaminants, providing highly purified water. Each type caters to specific filtration needs and quality requirements.</p></p>
