<p><p>Cardiac Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) is an advanced diagnostic tool used to visualize the heart's structure and function. The market is segmented into closed and open systems. Closed system MRI refers to traditional, high-field machines that provide superior image quality but require the patient to be fully enclosed, which can induce anxiety. Open system MRI offers a more spacious design, reducing claustrophobia, but typically delivers lower resolution images. Both systems serve different patient needs and diagnostic requirements.</p></p>
