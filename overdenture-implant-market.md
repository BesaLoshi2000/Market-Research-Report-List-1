<p><p>The overdenture implant market primarily consists of two types: tooth-supported overdentures and implant-supported overdentures. Tooth-supported overdentures rely on existing natural teeth for stability and support, offering a less invasive option for patients with some remaining healthy teeth. In contrast, implant-supported overdentures utilize dental implants inserted into the jawbone for enhanced stability and retention, making them suitable for patients with significant tooth loss. Both options aim to improve function, aesthetics, and overall quality of life for denture wearers.</p></p>
