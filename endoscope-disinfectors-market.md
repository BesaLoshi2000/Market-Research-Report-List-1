<p><strong>The Endoscope Disinfectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Multi Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Endoscope disinfectors are essential for maintaining hygiene in medical settings. The market is primarily divided into two types: single chamber and multi-chamber disinfectors. Single chamber disinfectors utilize one chamber for both cleaning and disinfecting, providing a compact solution suitable for smaller facilities. In contrast, multi-chamber disinfectors consist of separate chambers for cleaning, rinsing, and disinfection, allowing for a more thorough and efficient process, ideal for larger healthcare facilities that handle numerous endoscopes regularly. Both types ensure effective reprocessing of medical instruments.</p></p>
