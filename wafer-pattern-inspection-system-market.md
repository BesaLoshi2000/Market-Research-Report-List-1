<p><p>The Wafer Pattern Inspection System market encompasses various technologies used to detect defects in semiconductor manufacturing. The Electron Beam Inspection System utilizes focused electron beams for high-resolution imaging, making it ideal for identifying small defects. The Bright-Field Inspection System illuminates the wafer, highlighting defects against a bright background for easy detection. In contrast, the Dark-Field Inspection System uses oblique lighting to emphasize defects against a dark background, enhancing visibility of surface anomalies. Each system serves specific needs in quality control processes.</p></p>
