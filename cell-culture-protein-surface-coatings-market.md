<p><p>The Cell Culture Protein Surface Coatings market comprises two main types: self-coating and pre-coating. Self-coating involves surfaces that can spontaneously form a protein layer when in contact with cell culture media, promoting cell attachment and growth. In contrast, pre-coating refers to surfaces that are intentionally coated with proteins before cell culture use, ensuring a controlled and consistent environment for cell proliferation. Both methods aim to enhance cellular behavior and improve experimental reliability in biotechnological applications.</p></p>
