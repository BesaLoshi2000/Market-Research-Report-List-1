<p><p>The milk refrigeration tank market comprises two main types: closed-type and open-type tanks. Closed-type tanks are fully sealed, providing better temperature control and preventing contamination, making them ideal for large-scale dairy operations. Open-type tanks, on the other hand, have an open design that facilitates easy access for cleaning and maintenance but may be less effective in maintaining optimal temperatures. Both types serve the essential function of cooling and preserving milk quality before processing or distribution.</p></p>
