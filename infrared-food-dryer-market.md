<p><ul><li>Continuous Type</li><li>Discontinuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared food dryer market consists of two primary types: continuous and discontinuous. Continuous type dryers operate continuously, allowing for a steady flow of food processing, making them ideal for large-scale production with high efficiency. In contrast, discontinuous type dryers process food in batches, offering flexibility and easier handling for smaller operations or varying production needs. Both types utilize infrared radiation to effectively remove moisture from food, enhancing preservation while maintaining quality and flavor.</p></p>
