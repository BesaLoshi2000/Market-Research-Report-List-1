<p><p>The Computed Tomography (CT) scan market comprises various types, including X-Ray CT, Ultrasonic CT, and Gamma Rays CT. X-Ray CT uses X-ray radiation to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body, commonly employed in medical diagnosis. Ultrasonic CT employs high-frequency sound waves for imaging, offering a non-invasive option for certain soft tissue evaluations. Gamma Rays CT utilizes gamma radiation, primarily in nuclear medicine, to visualize metabolic activity and diagnose conditions like cancer. Each type serves unique clinical applications and patient needs.</p></p>
