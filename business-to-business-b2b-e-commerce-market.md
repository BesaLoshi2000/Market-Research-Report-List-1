<p><p>Business-to-Business (B2B) e-commerce can be categorized into three main market types. Supplier-oriented e-commerce centers on manufacturers or wholesalers showcasing their goods to attract buyers, enhancing visibility and sales. Intermediary-oriented e-commerce involves third-party platforms that connect buyers and suppliers, streamlining transactions and logistics while providing a broader range of options for both parties.</p></p>
