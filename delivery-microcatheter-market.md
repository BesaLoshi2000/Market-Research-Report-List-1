<p><strong>The Delivery Microcatheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Lumen Microcatheters</li><li>Dual-Lumen Microcatheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The delivery microcatheter market comprises single-lumen and dual-lumen microcatheters, designed for specific clinical applications in minimally invasive procedures. Single-lumen microcatheters feature a single channel, allowing for straightforward delivery of therapeutic agents or contrast media. In contrast, dual-lumen microcatheters have two channels, enabling simultaneous delivery and aspiration, enhancing procedural efficiency and precision. Both types are essential in various medical fields, including interventional radiology, cardiology, and neurology, facilitating targeted treatments while minimizing trauma to surrounding tissues.</p></p>
