<p><p>Bakery premixes are categorized into three main market types: complete mixes, dough-base mixes, and dough concentrates. Complete mixes contain all necessary ingredients for baking, offering convenience for users. Dough-base mixes provide key components like flour and sugar, requiring minimal additional ingredients, allowing for customization. Dough concentrates, on the other hand, include partially processed dough, enabling bakers to save time while still producing fresh products. Each type caters to different baking needs and preferences, enhancing efficiency in the baking process.</p></p>
