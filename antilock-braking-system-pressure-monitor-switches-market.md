<p><p>The Antilock Braking System (ABS) pressure monitor switches market includes normally closed and normally open switch types. Normally closed switches maintain electrical continuity under standard conditions, closing the circuit to signal normal brake pressure. In contrast, normally open switches do not conduct electricity unless triggered by specific pressure thresholds, indicating a fault or change in brake pressure. These switches play a crucial role in ensuring vehicle safety by providing real-time feedback on braking system performance and stability.</p></p>
