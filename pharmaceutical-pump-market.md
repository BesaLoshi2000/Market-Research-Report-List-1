<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li><li>Jet Pump</li><li>Air Lift Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical pump market comprises various types tailored for fluid handling in production and research. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to move liquids, suitable for high-flow applications. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, move fixed volumes per cycle, ideal for precise dosing. Jet pumps utilize high-pressure fluid jets to lift or transfer liquids, while air lift pumps employ air to move fluids upwards. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring efficient, reliable fluid management in pharmaceutical processes.</p></p>
